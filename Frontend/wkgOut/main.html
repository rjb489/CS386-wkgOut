This is wkgOut

wkgOut was created as web app designed to help people who enjoy perfecting and improving in workout sessions. wkgOut is a journaling and workout tracking tool that allows users to build workouts from pre-sets or original ideas, and use a journaling system to document and/or reflect on sessions. The work out building tool will also feature the ability to share the workout with other users.
